Become Our
Moderator

Benefits

  • Enjoy unrestricted access to the app's features.
  • Play a key role in shaping the community by monitoring updates and activities.
  • Control membership and uphold community standards.

Eligibility

  • Active app user with a passion for community growth.
  • Ability to maintain objectivity and adhere to guidelines.
  • Basic understanding of content moderation principles.

Guidelines

  • Maintain a respectful and inclusive environment for all users.
  • Ensure collections adhere to community policies.
  • Report violations promptly and handle disputes fairly.

Roles & Responsibility

  • Review and approve new collections submitted by users.
  • Monitor shared content for policy compliance.
  • Collaborate with the admin team to improve moderation processes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any training provided for new moderators?
Yes, new moderators may receive guidance or access to resources to understand community policies, tools, and best practices.
Is this a paid role or voluntary?
Moderation roles are typically voluntary and aimed at those passionate about community building, though perks like unrestricted app access are provided.
Can I apply if I have no prior moderation experience?
Yes, a passion for community growth and a willingness to learn are more important than prior experience.
Can I step down as a moderator if I am unable to continue?
Yes, you can notify the admin team if you wish to step down, and your contributions will be appreciated.
How much time commitment is expected from moderators?
While there are no strict time requirements, moderators should dedicate enough time to effectively monitor and manage the community.
