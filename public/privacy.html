Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Velarudh Infotech Private Limited ("we", "our", or "Company") collects, uses, and protects your personal information through our platform 5BestInCity.com and related services.

This Privacy Policy is published in accordance with the provisions of Rule 3(1) of the Information Technology (Intermediaries Guidelines) Rules, 2011, and the Information Technology Act, 2000. This policy complies with applicable Indian data protection laws and regulations.

Your information is used for:
